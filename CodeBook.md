## Identifiers
- subject_id - ID of the test subject, 1 to 30
- activity_id - Activity label, 1 to 6

## Activity label

- 2 WALKING_UPSTAIRS
- 3 WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
- 4 SITTING
- 5 STANDING
- 6 LAYING
